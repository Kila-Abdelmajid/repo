    Matto Essaouira is a charming restaurant located in the coastal town of Essaouira, Morocco. It offers a delightful fusion of Italian and Moroccan cuisine, blending traditional flavors with modern twists. The ambiance is warm and inviting, with a mix of traditional Moroccan decor and contemporary touches. Guests can enjoy a diverse menu featuring freshly prepared seafood, flavorful tagines, pasta dishes, and wood-fired pizzas. The restaurant's rooftop terrace provides stunning views of the historic medina and the Atlantic Ocean, making it an ideal spot for a memorable dining experience. Whether you're craving authentic Moroccan specialties or Italian classics, Matto Essaouira promises a culinary journey that delights the senses.
